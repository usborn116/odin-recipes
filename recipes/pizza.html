Pizza

Description

Pizza is a perfect start-of-the-weekend food!

This recipe is easy to follow and quick to make

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Roll out the dough
  2. Put the sauce on the dough, then top with the cheese and pepperoni
  3. Bake at 450 degrees for 20 minutes
    4. >
  4. Serve!
