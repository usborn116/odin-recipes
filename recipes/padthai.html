Pad Thai

Description

Pad thai is a sweet, savory noodle dish that is a crowd favorite!

This recipe is easy to follow and quick to make

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Sriracha
  • Rice Noodles
  • Thai Chilis
  • Bell Pepper

Steps

  1. Add the peanut butter, sriracha, thai chilis in a pot and heat
  2. Add in the noodles and bell pepper once the liquid ingredients are mixed and heated
  3. Serve!
